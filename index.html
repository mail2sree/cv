Srila Prabhupada

Founder Acharya of The International Socity of Krishna Consciousness

Abhay Charanaravinda Bhaktivedanta Swami or Srila Prabhupada, born Abhay
Charan De, was an Indian spiritual teacher and the founder-acharya of the
International Society for Krishna Consciousness, commonly known as the
"Hare Krishna movement".
